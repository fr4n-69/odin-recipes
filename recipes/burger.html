Textured Soy Protein Burger

Description

A protein and fast-cooking meal to eat after training hard.

Ingredients

To make this homemade burger we will need:

  • 250 gr of TSP
  • 1 tsp. of onion powder
  • 1 tsp. of garlic powder
  • 1 cup of chickpea flour
  • 1 tsp. of Agar Agar (vegan jelly)
  • 1/2 of cucumber
  • Some leaves of arugula
  • 1 tomato
  • 2 burger bread

Steps

  1. Hydrate the TSP.
  2. Then mix it with the onion powder, the garlic powder, the cup of chickpea flour. You could season to taste by adding salt or pepper.
  3. Finally add the Agar Agar to give it consistency.
  4. Once it has been mixed, make burgers of 100gr each.
  5. Cook the burgers.
  6. Cut in slices the tomato and the cucumber.
  7. Put the cooked burger in its breads, add the tomato and cucumber slices and some arugula leaves.
  8. Enjoy your vegan and healthy burger!
