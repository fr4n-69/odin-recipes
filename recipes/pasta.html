Pasta with vegan mushroom sauce

Description

It is an incredible warm dish to eat during this cold winter on Sunday's family meal.

Ingredients

We need the following ingredients:

  • 1kg of pasta
  • A can of mushrooms
  • Some leaves of cilantro
  • 250ml of vegan milk or water
  • 1 cup of chickpea flour

Steps

  1. Blend the half of the mushrooms with the 250ml of vegan milk/water.
  2. After blending, add the chickpea flour and mix it to make it creamy.
  3. Pour the mix into a pot and simmer. Then add the other half of mushrooms.
  4. Cook the pasta while the sauce is being cooked.
  5. When the pasta is done, mix it with the sauce and add some leaves of cilantro to add flavor.
  6. Eat!
